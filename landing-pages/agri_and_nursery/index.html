Our Story

Farming the Old Way,
For a New Generation

ROOTED started with a simple belief: food should come from soil, not factories. Our founders left desk jobs to learn the craft of sustainable agriculture, and we've been growing ever since.

We partner with small family farms within 50 miles of our packing facility. Every box you receive supports not just our farm, but a network of growers committed to regenerative practices.
